Rules for Conways Game of Life

In the Game of Life, these rules examine each cell of the grid. For each cell, it counts that cell's neighbors, and then act on that result.

1. If the cell is alive and has two or three neighbors, then it remains alive. Else it dies.

2. If the cell is dead and has exactly three neighbors, then it comes to life. Else if remains dead.
